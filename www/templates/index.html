The geolocation model is able to localize over 72 world regions as shown in the map.

The current geolocation approach follows a hierarchical generative model that builds cells depending on the distribution of the training dataset. For this reason, every cell contains between 3k and 30k images.

  • The test performance reached by this model are

    Top 1: 14.31%

    Top 5: 32.79%

    Top 10: 45.95%

  • The top validation performance are

    Validation loss: 4.8

    Validation accuracy: 0.25
